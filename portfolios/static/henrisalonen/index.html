About

I have been a musician practically all my life, but, I really sold my soul to the devil at the crossroads 18 years ago. Now, playing various instruments for number of years does not make one a fantastic film composer on its own, a good set of ears and an in depth understanding and feel for emotional content is a big part of the job description.
‘Simplicity is the final achievement. After one has played a vast quantity of notes and more notes, it is simplicity that emerges as the crowning reward of art.’ Frederic Chopin
